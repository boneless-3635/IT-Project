Product

With the current COVID situation where people are concerned about getting infected from contacting the outer source, the level of sensitivity for sanitization is very high. As important as it is to stay safe from germs, being hydrated is also as important. Our team, AutoWater, has decided to figure out a system that can aid us to be both hydrated and healthy from infections. In troducing the auto water filling dispenser machine.

Our machine can fill a standardized cup that can hold at least 300 ml of liquid. Just place a cup into the filling platform, The Auto Water Dispenser will detect and fill it with liquid until 300ml.
